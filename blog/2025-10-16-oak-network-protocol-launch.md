## What is Oak Network?

Oak Network is a decentralized crowdfunding infrastructure built on the Celo blockchain that serves as the foundation for all applications, crowdfunding-specific or not, to leverage the power of blockchain technology for fundraising with a transparent 1% fee that goes directly to the ecosystem.

## Smart Contracts Deployed

Our core smart contracts are now live on Celo:

- **CampaignInfoFactory**: Creates and manages campaign instances
- **CampaignInfo**: Stores campaign metadata and configuration
- **GlobalParams**: Manages protocol-wide parameters and fees
- **TreasuryFactory**: Deploys appropriate treasury contracts
- **AllOrNothing**: "All or nothing" funding model implementation
