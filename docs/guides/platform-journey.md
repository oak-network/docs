# Platform Journey: Why Choose Oak Network

Transform your platform into a powerful crowdfunding ecosystem with Oak Network's comprehensive infrastructure. This guide explains the key benefits and strategic advantages of integrating with our decentralized crowdfunding protocol.

## Overview

Oak Network provides the infrastructure that enables any platform to offer crowdfunding capabilities without building everything from scratch. By integrating with our smart contracts, platforms can focus on their core value proposition while leveraging battle-tested, secure, and transparent crowdfunding infrastructure.

## Key Benefits for Platforms

### üåê Cross-Platform Campaign Sharing

**Expand Your Reach Globally**

Enable your creators to launch campaigns on your platform while automatically sharing them across other platforms worldwide. This creates a network effect that benefits everyone:

- **For Creators**: Global exposure and access to diverse funding sources
- **For Platforms**: Revenue sharing from cross-platform campaigns
- **For Backers**: Access to campaigns from platforms they might not have discovered otherwise

**How It Works:**
- Creators launch campaigns on your platform
- Campaigns are automatically shared with partner platforms
- Each platform gets their configured fee percentage
- Backers can contribute from any participating platform
- Funds are pooled and managed transparently

**Revenue Model:**
- Platform hosting fee: 1-3% (configurable)
- Protocol fee: 1% (fixed)
- Creator receives: 96-98% of total funds raised

## Platform Integration Journey

### Phase 1: Planning and Setup

**1. Platform Assessment**
- Evaluate current user base and technical capabilities
- Determine integration level (Basic, Hybrid, or Full Crypto)
- Identify key stakeholders and decision makers

**2. Technical Planning**
- Review Oak Network smart contracts
- Plan frontend integration approach
- Design user experience flows

**3. Legal and Compliance**
- Review regulatory requirements
- Understand fee structures and revenue sharing
- Plan compliance documentation

### Phase 2: Development and Testing

**1. Smart Contract Integration**
- Deploy and configure Oak Network contracts
- Implement campaign creation and management
- Set up treasury and fee distribution

**2. Frontend Development**
- Build campaign creation interfaces
- Implement contribution flows
- Design campaign discovery and browsing

**3. Testing and Quality Assurance**
- Test all integration points
- Verify fee calculations and distributions
- Conduct user acceptance testing

### Phase 3: Launch and Optimization

**1. Soft Launch**
- Launch with limited creator base
- Monitor system performance
- Gather user feedback

**2. Full Launch**
- Open to all creators
- Implement marketing campaigns
- Monitor key metrics

**3. Optimization**
- Analyze usage patterns
- Optimize user experience
- Scale infrastructure as needed
